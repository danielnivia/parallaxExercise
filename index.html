Welcome to San Gerardo de Dota

San Gerardo de Dota is a small village located in the mountain range of Talamanca in Costa Rica. It takes around 2 hours drive from the capital, San Jose. Minis, will be your guide during the rest of the trip, she enjoys company to walk. Did you see the quetzal bird? This region is famous for the sightings of this beautiful bird, distinguished by its green feathers and red chest.

Fish swiming and jumping down the river

The Savegre river passes through San Gerardo de Dota. This pristine river provides to some relaxing sounds that help you escape from the city soundscape. This region is famous for fish farms of rainbow trout. Most restaurants and hotel serve this delicious fish in diverse kinds of ways.
